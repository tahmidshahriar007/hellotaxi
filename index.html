How can I book a taxi?

You can book a taxi by phone, through a ride-sharing app, or online. A deposit may be required for certain bookings. Payment methods typically include cash, credit cards, and mobile payments. Foreign credit cards are usually accepted.

Do I need to pay a deposit to book a taxi?

It depends on the taxi company and the type of service you're booking. Some companies may require a deposit, especially for long-distance or special event bookings.
